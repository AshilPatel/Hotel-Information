Jaisalmer Marriott Resort & Spa

Jaisalmer-Sam-Dhanana Road, Police Line, 345001 Jaisalmer, India

Price:- 3,000₹-6,000₹ /per night

Description:-


Built in yellow sandstone architecture in Jaisalmer, 1.4 km from Jaisalmer Fort, Jaisalmer Marriott Resort & Spa features a temperature controlled swimming pool, a 24 hours fitness centre and an outdoor lawn, as well as free WiFi.


Rooms are complete with 3 fixture bathroom while certain units at Jaisalmer Marriott Resort & Spa also boast a seating area. At the accommodation, every room comes with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels. Ironing facilities and hairdryer are available.


The property has three dining options - The Jaisalmer Kitchen: All day dining restaurant that features Indian and international cuisines, The Mithai Company - Sweet & savories counter with fine selection of teas and coffees. Wyra: Rooftop restaurant operational only at dinner, offers grills and live cooking. Dunes Bar – offering signature cocktail stations and a range of exclusive wines.


At Jaisalmer Marriott Resort & Spa guests are welcome to take advantage of a sauna, steam and personalized therapies at Quan Spa. Play and enjoy games at Entertainment Executive Lounge - Shuffle. Guests can make use of the business centre to work or head for a sightseeing trip organized by the tour desk.


The property is an 18-minute walk from Patwon Ki Haveli, 2.5 km from Lake Gadisar and 5 km from Bara Baag. The hotel has textile and handloom shopping centre and 3 meeting spaces. The nearest airport is Jaisalmer Airport, 14.9 km from the accommodation.


Couples particularly like the location — they rated it 9.0 for a two-person trip.

Hotel Naveen Lakeside

Unkal Lake, 580025 Hubli, India

Price:- 3,000₹-8,000₹ /per night

Description:-


Spreading across 9 acres of lush greenery, Hotel Naveen Lakeside is conveniently situated within 1 km from Unkal Lake. It features an outdoor pool, a business centre, a 24-hour front desk and free parking on site.


The hotel is just 4 km to the well-known ISKCON Temple and 5 km to Hubli city centre. Hubli Junction Railway Station is about 6 km away, while Hubli Airport is accessible within 8 km.


Fitted with tiled flooring, air-conditioned rooms are well-equipped with a personal safe, a flat-screen satellite TV, a wardrobe and seating area. Tea/coffee making facilities and a minibar are also included. The en suite bathroom comes with bathrobes, shower and free toiletries. Free Wifi is available in guest rooms.


Hotel Naveen Lakeside offers currency exchange, laundry services and meeting/banqueting facilities upon request. Guests may rent a car to explore the area, enjoy a massage or work out at the gym. The tour desk can assist with sightseeing and travel arrangements.


The in-house restaurant, Buzz, serves delectable Indian, Continental and Chinese dishes.


Couples particularly like the location — they rated it 8.5 for a two-person trip.

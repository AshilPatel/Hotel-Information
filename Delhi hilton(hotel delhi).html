Hilton Garden Inn New Delhi/Saket

A4, DLF Avenue Saket District Centre, Saket, 110017 New Delhi, India

Price:- 3,000₹-6,000₹ /per night

Description:-


Hilton Garden Inn Saket is located in DLF Avenue Mall in Saket, South Delhi. Located 500 m from corporate hub in Saket District Centre, 1 km from Saket District Court. 2 minutes walk to Select City Walk Mall. In close proximity to UNESCO World Heritage site, Qutub Institutional area, Okhla Industrial area. Nearby medical facilities includes Max Super Speciality hospital-Saket , PSRI and Rainbow Children Hospital.


It offers free Wi-Fi, an outdoor swimming pool, and a 24-hour business centre. All well-equipped rooms offer city/mall view. The air conditioned guest rooms are equipped with a work desk,Ergonomic chair, flat-screen and a mini fridge. Tea/coffee making facilities, 24 hour front desk and laundry services are also available.


Guests can enjoy multi-cuisine flavours at India Grill Restaurant and a drink in the High Spirits bar and lounge. Meeting room facility is available.


The nearest metro station is Malviya Nagar Metro station which is 1.5 km away.Indira Gandhi International Airport is 14 km from the property.


Couples particularly like the location — they rated it 9.1 for a two-person trip.

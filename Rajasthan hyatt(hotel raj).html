Hyatt Regency Jaipur Mansarovar

ISCKON, Mansarovare, 302020 Jaipur, India

Price:- 4,000₹-7,000₹ /per night

Description:-


Situated in Jaipur, 3.4 km from Govind Dev Ji Temple, Hyatt Regency Jaipur Mansarovar features accommodation with a restaurant, free private parking, an outdoor swimming pool and a fitness centre. 7 km from Birla Mandir Temple, Jaipur and 9 km from Sindhi Camp, the property offers a bar and a garden. The accommodation provides a 24-hour front desk, room service and currency exchange for guests.


At the hotel rooms are equipped with air conditioning, a seating area, a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a safety deposit box and a private bathroom with a shower, bathrobes and slippers. All rooms are equipped with a kettle, while some rooms include a balcony and others also boast garden views. At Hyatt Regency Jaipur Mansarovar all rooms include bed linen and towels.


The accommodation offers a continental or buffet breakfast.


Hyatt Regency Jaipur Mansarovar offers a terrace.


Jantar Mantar, Jaipur is 10 km from the hotel, while City Palace is 10 km from the property. The nearest airport is Jaipur International, 5 km from Hyatt Regency Jaipur Mansarovar, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.


Couples particularly like the location — they rated it 8.9 for a two-person trip.

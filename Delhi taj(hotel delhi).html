The Taj Mahal Hotel New Delhi

PNo 1 Mansingh Road, 110011 New Delhi, India

Price:- 6,000₹-8,000₹ /per night

Description:-


Located in the heart of Lutyen’s Delhi, Taj Mahal Hotel New Delhi offers luxurious accommodation situated amidst 6 acres of landscaped grounds. Featuring 7 dining options, 5-star facilities include a state-of-the-art fitness centre, an outdoor pool and spa.


Taj Mahal Hotel New Delhi is within 2 km from the well-known Rashtrapati Bhavan. Offering free parking, it is 18 km from the Delhi International Airport and 5 km New Delhi Railway Station. The Central Business District, the shopping area of Connaught Place and the business district of Gurgaon are within a 25-minute drive.


Featuring modern Indian-style decor, luxurious rooms are equipped with fax machines and flat-screen cable TVs. They come with a safe and a sofa at the comfortable seating area. All rooms come with a view.


After a day of activity, enjoy soothing massages at Taj Mahal Hotel New Delhi’s The Spa, a steam bath or a beauty treatment at the salon. Staff can attend to travel, childcare and banking needs. The concierge can facilitate a Heritage Walk to Humayun's Tomb, a wellness walk to Lodi Garden and a rickshaw tour of Old Delhi. Guests can also enjoy the experience of Change of Guard Ceremony at the Rashtrapati Bhavan.


Dining options Varq that serves modern Indian delights and Machan the international eatery. A selection of Chinese cuisine can be enjoyed at the House of Ming. Refreshing beverages can be enjoyed at the Emperor Lounge and Ricks.


Couples particularly like the location — they rated it 9.3 for a two-person trip.

The Leela Palace Bengaluru

23 Old Airport Road, 560008 Bangalore, India

Price:- 15,000₹-22,000₹ /per night

Description:-


Nestled in the midst of lush gardens that spread across 7 acres, The Leela Palace Bangalore features décor reflecting the grandeur of the bygone era and boasts of facilities such as an outdoor swimming pool, a spa and wellness centre, Yoga studio and 4 award winning food and beverage / dining options. Free private parking is provided.


Rooms at Leela Palace Bangalore are elegantly designed and feature an LCD TV with DVD player, minibar and tea/coffee maker. 24-hour room service is available.


The Leela Palace's spa offers wide selection of treatments and massages. The spa also features a fitness centre, sauna, steam room and hot tub.


Guests can head to Citrus, an all day dining restaurant, to relish Asian, Mediterranean, European and Indian cuisine, Jamavar - Specialty Indian Restaurant, to enjoy Indian delicacies. Japanese, Chinese, Korean and Thai cuisines are offered at Zen - the Pan Asian specialty restaurant while French and Italian dishes can be relished at Le Cirque Signature. Wide array of alcoholic and non-alcoholic beverages can be enjoyed at Library Bar.


The Leela Palace Bengaluru is located on Hal Airport Road and is minutes away from the Embassy Golf Links IT Park, Bagmane Tech Park and has easy access to all other business parks on Outer Ring Road, with close proximity to aeronautical companies such as ISRO and aeronautical Development Authority. 15 minutes away from Shopping and Entertainment Hub at Indiranagar and 3 km away from City Centre / MG Road. Located Close to KGA Golf Course. Distance from the Leela Palace Bangalore to the international and domestic airports is 45 km.


Couples particularly like the location — they rated it 9.0 for a two-person trip.
